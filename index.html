Privacy Policy — CSV Whisperer

Last updated: April 4, 2026

CSV Whisperer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Shopify application that helps merchants import products from CSV and Excel files. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

When you install and use CSV Whisperer, we collect and store the following information:

  • Store domain — your Shopify store URL (e.g. your-store.myshopify.com), used for authentication and to associate import history with your store.
  • Import history — the file name, date, total number of rows, and how many products were successfully imported or failed. We do not store the actual file contents.
  • Session data — standard Shopify authentication tokens required for the app to function.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not store the contents of uploaded CSV or Excel files. Files are processed in your browser and sent directly to the Shopify API.
  • We do not collect any customer personal data from your store.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. How we use your data

The data we collect is used solely to provide the app's functionality — specifically, to display your import history and to authenticate your store with the Shopify API.

4. Data retention

Import history records are stored for as long as the app is installed on your store. When you uninstall the app, your data is deleted within 48 hours.

5. Third parties

CSV Whisperer uses the Shopify API to create products in your store. No data is shared with any other third parties.

6. Your rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or uninstalling the app. To request data deletion, please contact us at the email address below.
